# Commodity Market Research

Independent quantitative research on commodity markets, with a focus on price dynamics,
macroeconomic drivers, and data-driven analysis.

## Scope
This repository consolidates:
- curated data sources for commodity markets
- replication of academic and applied research
- original research projects
- exploratory analyses and early-stage ideas

## Repository Structure

- `sources/`  
  Curated links and descriptions of data sources (EIA, FRED, World Bank, etc.)

- `replications/`  
  Replication of academic and applied research papers, with notes on methodology
  and reproducibility.

- `research/`  
  Original research projects, including research questions, analysis, and conclusions.

- `explorations/`  
  Early-stage ideas, experiments, and informal analyses.

- `src/`  
  Reusable utility functions and helpers.

## Tools
- Python
- pandas, numpy
- statsmodels, scikit-learn
- PyTorch
- R
- MATLAB
- Jupyter notebooks
